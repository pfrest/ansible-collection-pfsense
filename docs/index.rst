pfREST Ansible Documentation
============================

Welcome to the pfREST Ansible documentation!

pfREST is a community-driven effort dedicated to enhancing the capabilities of pfSense Community Edition (CE). Our
primary goal is to bridge the gap in missing modern features, ensuring that pfSense CE remains a powerful and viable
firewall solution for home labs, hobbyists, and organizations alike.
